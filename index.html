About E-vide

E-vide is a revolutionary platform designed to bring trust and transparency to online transactions. Our mission is to eliminate fraud and ensure that what you see online is exactly what you get.

With our dual verification system, both buyers and sellers can transact with confidence, knowing that products have been independently verified.

How E-vide Works

Step 1: First Verification

Seller uploads product photos and details, generating a unique verification link.

Step 2: Second Verification

Independent verifier confirms product condition with real-time photos.

Step 3: Confirmation

Buyer receives verified product details and can proceed with confidence.

Step 4: Secure Transaction

Complete the purchase knowing exactly what you're getting.
